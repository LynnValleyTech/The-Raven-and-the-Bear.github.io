Our Story

For 16 years, The Raven and the Bear Arts has been proudly showcasing the finest Northwest Coast and local native artworks in Vancouver, BC.

Founded in 2008, our gallery has become a destination for those seeking authentic First Nations art, from hand-carved wooden plaques and masks to exquisite silver jewelry and BC jade creations.

We work directly with artists to bring you pieces that honor tradition while embracing contemporary expressions of First Nations culture.
